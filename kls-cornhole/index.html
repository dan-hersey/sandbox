By The Numbers

Stats & Records

👑
The GOAT
Joey Kiedaisch — 5 Titles
The all-time leader with 5 championships. Joey dominated the early years and keeps adding to his legacy. Mike Hersey is right behind with 4.
🔥
Mr. Fourth of July
Dan Hersey
All 3 of Dan's titles have come on Independence Day — 2021, 2023, and 2025. The undisputed king of the Fourth.
🎆
Fourth of July Specialist
Louie Grasso
All 3 of Louie's titles also came on the Fourth — 2020, 2023, and 2024. Two players, same holiday dominance.
💪
Labor Day King
Mike Hersey & Ian O'Rourke
Both have 2 Labor Day titles. Mike won 2022 & 2023, Ian won 2021 & 2024. Nobody owns Monday quite like these two.
👨‍👦
Family Affair
Mike & Dan Hersey
Father-in-law and son-in-law teamed up to take the Fourth of July 2025 title — the first family pair to share a championship.
👪
Hersey Dynasty
8 titles across 3 family members
Mike (4), wife Celia (1), and son-in-law Dan (3) combine for 8 championship titles — the most dominant family in KLS history.
🏘️
Grasso Household
5 titles across 3 family members
Louie (3), Cassie (1), and Dawn (1) represent Wilmington, MA well with 5 combined championships.
🗺️
Massachusetts Dominance
~60% of titles
Players from the Bay State — Westminster, Wilmington, Peabody, Billerica, Norwell — have claimed the lion's share of championships.
🎲
Random Draw Magic
0 repeat pairings*
With randomly drawn teams, no championship-winning duo has won together twice — every title goes to a unique pairing. (*pending full records)
