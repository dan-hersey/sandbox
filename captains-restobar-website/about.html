Our Story

More Than a Restaurant

A waterfront tradition on Greenwich Bay.

A Place on the Water

Captain's Resto-Bar was built around a simple idea: bring people together on the water. For years, Greenwich Bay was just a beautiful backdrop, but it deserved a gathering place. A place where locals could come together, where neighbors become friends, and where every sunset feels like a celebration.

Every summer, we open the doors and something magical happens. The patio fills up with familiar faces and new ones. Cold drinks find their way into eager hands. The bay becomes the backdrop for unforgettable nights, laughter that echoes off the water, and memories that stick with you long after the season ends.

It started as a simple idea — great food, honest drinks, and the best view in Warwick. But it became so much more. It became a waterfront tradition, a seasonal gathering spot where locals come for fresh food, cold drinks, and good company. This is more than a restaurant. This is home on the water.

What Makes Us Different

The Food

We don't cut corners. Our house-smoked brisket takes hours. Our New England seafood comes fresh from trusted local suppliers. Everything that leaves our kitchen is made with care and intention. We know our ingredients, we respect our recipes, and we never settle for less than fresh.

The Location

There's no view quite like Greenwich Bay at sunset. From every corner of our patio, you're watching the water change colors, the light dance across the bay, and the day ease into evening. This isn't just a meal — it's an experience, and the bay is part of the story.

The Vibe

We keep things relaxed. Live music, cold drinks, and genuine hospitality create an atmosphere where everyone belongs. Whether you're in flip-flops, work clothes, or a blazer, you're welcome here. We're not pretentious. We're just here to host good meals and better memories.

What We Stand For

Everyone's Welcome
